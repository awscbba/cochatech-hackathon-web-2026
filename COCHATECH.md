1. Executive Summary

COCHATECH 2026 is a large-scale open innovation hackathon that connects multidisciplinary university talent with real challenges from the banking, business, and social sectors.

It transforms creative energy into validated, implementable technological solutions through a structured 12-week operational process.

Key Facts

Date: May 2026 (36–48 intensive hours)

Location: Cochabamba, Bolivia

Target registrations: 500 students

Preselected teams: 35 multidisciplinary teams

Methodology: Innovation Challenges + Design Thinking

Total challenges: 9

3 Business

3 Banking

3 Social

2. Strategic Objectives
Core Purpose

To align universities, banks, companies, NGOs, and technology partners under a structured governance model that produces viable technological solutions with real-world implementation potential.

Strategic Goals

Connect university students to real industry and social challenges.

Develop innovative and technically viable solutions.

Foster multidisciplinary collaboration.

Generate solutions with real implementation potential.

Strengthen the regional innovation ecosystem.

3. 12-Week Integrated Timeline
Phase 1 – Diagnosis & Call (Weeks 1–4)

Validate 9 official challenges.

Reach 500+ student registrations.

Launch marketing campaign.

Collect complete Challenge Documentation Forms.

Phase 2 – Preselection (Weeks 5–6)

Close registrations.

Conduct networking sessions.

Select 35 multidisciplinary teams.

Assign each team to a specific challenge.

Phase 3 – Preparation & Mentorship (Weeks 7–9)

Virtual kickoff.

Mandatory bootcamps:

Open Innovation & Design Thinking

Business Model Canvas

Cloud & APIs

Social Impact Measurement

Deep immersion sessions with challenge owners.

Target: 80% attendance in training sessions.

Phase 4 – Intensive Preparation (Weeks 10–11)

Deliver datasets and APIs.

Q&A with banks and NGOs.

NDA signing (if applicable).

Finalize venue logistics, internet, audiovisual, catering.

Confidential document delivery

Team development sessions

Mentor checkpoints

Optional overnight work

Day 2
