# Gemini Image Request — COCHATECH 2026 Hero Image

## Concept
Cristo de la Concordia (the iconic Christ statue of Cochabamba, Bolivia) reimagined as a
futuristic tech symbol, surrounded by glowing hexagonal sponsor tiles, set against a dark
cinematic background that matches the website's midnight blue theme.

---

## Target Use
- Hero section right column (desktop: ~600x700px display area)
- Must work on dark backgrounds (#020b1c deep midnight blue)
- Should feel premium, cinematic, and tech-forward

---

## Prompt (use this exactly or adapt)

```
Cinematic 3D render of Cristo de la Concordia statue from Cochabamba, Bolivia, reimagined
as a futuristic tech deity. The statue is dark metallic/obsidian material with glowing cyan
and electric blue circuit board traces running across the robe and body, emitting soft light.
Dramatic upward rim lighting in cyan (#00d2ff) and deep blue.

Surrounding the base: organized tiers of glowing hexagonal gems/tokens — gold ones at the
front and largest, then progressively smaller green, purple, blue, red, and silver hexagons
arranged in a deliberate arc behind. Each hexagon has a glowing inner light and beveled edge.

Circuit board trace lines extend outward on the dark ground from the base, like a PCB board.
Subtle light beams or data stream particles rise upward from the statue's outstretched hands.

Background: very dark midnight blue to black (#020b1c), with a faint deep cyan atmospheric
glow emanating from behind the statue. No bright sky, no light backgrounds.

Style: high-end CGI, cinematic lighting, 8K render quality, dramatic shadows, tech-noir
aesthetic. Aspect ratio approximately 3:4 (portrait).
```
