# Image Request: circuit-board.png

**Output filename:** `circuit-board.png`
**Output folder:** `/gemini/images/`
**Then copy to:** `/public/images/circuit-board.png`

## Description

Generate a high-resolution (1600x900px or 16:9) digital circuit board illustration with the following characteristics:

- **Background:** Very dark navy blue (#030f1e to #051a30 gradient)
- **Circuit traces:** Thin, gold/amber colored PCB traces (lines with 90-degree turns, like a real circuit board). They should branch and spread organically across the right 60% of the image, converging toward the center-left. Traces should be subtle, not overpowering.
- **Glowing nodes:** Bright cyan/teal (#00e5ff) glowing dots at trace intersections. Some nodes should have a strong glow/bloom effect. A few nodes should be gold/amber colored for contrast.
- **Light beams:** A few vertical glowing cyan light beams or "data streams" rising upward from the circuit, like energy flowing through the board.
- **Binary text:** Very faint binary digits (0s and 1s) scattered on the far right edge, barely visible.
- **Atmosphere:** Futuristic, tech, dark, premium. Like a motherboard seen from above in a dark room with neon lighting.
- **Style:** Photorealistic or high-quality digital art, NOT flat/cartoon.
- **Composition:** The circuit should be denser on the right side and sparser toward the left, so it blends into a dark background on the left edge (left 20% should be nearly empty/dark).

This image will be used as the right-side hero background of a hackathon website, overlaid with a CSS gradient that fades it out on the left.
